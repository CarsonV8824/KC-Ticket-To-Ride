About Ticket to Ride

Ticket to Ride is a popular board game where players collect train cards to claim railway routes across a map. The goal is to complete destination tickets and earn the most points by the end of the game.

How to Play

  • Each player starts with a set number of train cards and destination tickets.
  • On your turn, you can either draw train cards, claim a route, or draw additional destination tickets.
  • Claiming routes requires you to have the appropriate number and color of train cards.
  • The game ends when a player has two or fewer trains left, and final scores are calculated based on completed routes and tickets.
